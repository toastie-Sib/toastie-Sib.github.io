My major was in Games and Interactive Media focusing on the Game Design area. 

I also graduated with a minor in Information Technology, along with taking courses to provide additional coding experience such as Intro to Programming I & II and Discrete Structures in Computer Science.



I have learned how to work with the Unity, Godot, and Unreal game engines. I am also able to code in C#, C++, and Python!
